Our Campus

Christian Outreach Church and School are located in scenic central Jefferson County, just six miles north of Hillsboro, near Highway 21.

Close by is the Covered Bridge State Park, which has one of only five remaining covered bridges in the state of Missouri.

Three main facilities serve the church, school, and other ministries of Christian Outreach Church. They are

  • The Fellowship Hall which has the Lamb’s Inn Coffee House downstairs, and upstairs banquet room
  • The Chapel, our worship center, a unique octagonal building with attached lobby and offices
  • The Educational Facility which houses both the Christian Outreach School (K-12)

In addition there are three private residences. One of them serves as the home of Pastor Nick; the second is the home of Steve and Cathy Miller, administrators of Christian Outreach School; and the other is a rental home.
