Some Ways You Can Volunteer Your Help

If any of these interest you, please contact Elizabeth using our contact info.

People like you have made COC possible. Growing numbers of dear saints, who have given of themselves and their substance are the reason we are here to serve. Opportunities to help abound.

Can you give some of your time with various projects and even ministries here? Do you like to pray? Check out how you can give your time.

Do you have special gifts or interests that you would like to see make a difference? Look under talents.

Finances are always a very important part of our needed support. Has God given you the "gift of giving" (Rom. 12:8), then look under treasure.

Time

Supplies Manager

Helps keep track of expendable materials such as cups, plates, flatware, toilet paper, and so on. Time required: about two hours per month. Training required.

Usher

Part of the welcoming ministry. Helps worshipers find a place to sit, distributes materials, opens doors. Time: about 5 hours per month. Training required.

Building Exterior Maintenance

General repair and painting. Lamp maintenance, relamp exterior fixtures. Time required: about two hours per week. Training required.

Landscaping

Design, installation, or maintenance of campus landscaping. This could potentially use the services of a dozen or more people.

Talent

Communion Server

In our Sunday Services we celebrate communion on the first and third Sunday of each month. Communion servers minister in one of three "communion stations." Formal membership in Christian Outreach Church and training is required.

Lector

Read a portion of Scripture as part of the Sunday service.

Why Donate?

The Lord has given us two main objectives: First, to educate children so they can give respectful and effective answers to the humanism so pervasive in our world, and second to equip them to become shining Christian lights in whatever vocation God gives them.

Fundraising and charitable donations are critically important to the continued impact of Christian Outreach School. Our first preference is "unrestricted donations," so we can designate the best places to direct all contributions. However, if you would like to designate your contribution to a particular purpose, please let us know. We are a fully tax-exempt, 501(c)(3) organization.

If you have a particular purpose in mind for your gift, the IRS does not want the purpose to appear on your check (i.e. the "memo line). They have ruled, however, that you may enclose a note stating your wishes. The wording they prefer is, "We are praying that this contribution will be used for X, Y, or Z." This does not violate their rule that deductible gifts must be a "disinterested charitable contribution" in which the giver retains no control of his gift. We are very open to new ideas that align with our mission.

Please consider donating a financial blessing to Christian Outreach School. Contact us if you would like to visit the school or simply to learn more about us.
