Tuition and Fees for 2017-2018

Monthly tuition is based on ten months, August to May.

Registration Fee

Annual fee of $75.00 PER FAMILY. Due with application. Non-refundable.

Pre-School Tuition (3 and 4-year-olds)

Two-Day (Monday and Wednesday)

$70.00/month plus $35.00 materials fee.

Three-Day (Monday, Wednesday, and Friday)

$95.00/month plus $40.00 materials fee.

Five-Day (Monday through Friday)

$150.00/month plus $60.00 materials fee.

Additional fees for special programs, materials or field trips may be collected.

Kindergarten Tuition (Age 5 by July 31)

Half-Day (7:45am-11:45am)

$175.00/month plus $115.00 materials fee

Full-Day (7:45am-3:00pm)

$250.00/month plus $165.00 materials fee

Elementary (1st through 6th Grades)

$280.00/month plus $200.00 materials fee

Secondary (7th through 12th Grades)

$340.00/month plus $200.00 materials fee

Additional fees for special programs, materials or field trips may be collected. Materials fees are due by June 1st or upon acceptance. Some courses (such as drama, art, cooking) may require a special fee. Graduating seniors will be assessed a graduation fee.

DISCOUNTS ON TUITION ARE AS FOLLOWS:

Second child: 10%, Third child: 15%, Fourth+: 20%

DISCOUNT FOR PAYMENT IN FULL BY AUGUST 1

5% of total tuition


Information on financial assistance, part-time enrollment, and payment policies is available upon request.
